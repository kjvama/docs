Writing Style
==================================================
Titles
Use descriptive titles.

Example:
How to Record a Bible Lecture

NOT

Bible Recording Method

--------------------------------------------------

Sentences

Keep sentences short.

One idea per paragraph.

Keep paragraphs visually clean and easy to scan.

Avoid long paragraphs.

Break text into smaller sections.

Prefer bullet lists over long paragraphs when appropriate.
